Why The Happiness School?

The Happiness School is an organization focused on helping beings find happiness within themselves. Happiness is the ultimate goal of every decision we make, yet most people struggle to find it. That’s because true happiness isn’t where people are searching for it. It isn’t outside — it’s discovered within.

At The Happiness School, we guide you on this journey by providing understanding of yourself, and the tools to make changes to be able to actually discover happiness. This will help you break free from emotional struggles and live a life full of real and lasting happiness.
